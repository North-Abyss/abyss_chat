# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 21, 2026

Welcome to Abyss Chat! Your privacy is not just a priority—it is the foundational design principle of this application.

## 1. Information We Collect
**Short answer: We don't.**
Abyss Chat is a decentralized, peer-to-peer (P2P) communication application. We do not operate central servers to route, store, or monitor your messages, calls, or files. 
- **No Accounts Required:** We do not collect names, phone numbers, or email addresses.
- **No Data Harvesting:** We do not track analytics, telemetry, or behavioral data.

## 2. How Your Data is Handled
All communication in Abyss Chat flows directly between you and the person you are communicating with via WebRTC Data Channels.
- **End-to-End Encryption:** All traffic (text, files, and video) is cryptographically secured by the WebRTC protocol using DTLS and SRTP.
- **Local Storage:** Your profile, generated ID, and chat history are encrypted and stored **exclusively** on your local device. We have no access to it.
- **Signaling:** A lightweight signaling server is temporarily used solely to exchange connection handshakes (SDP offers/answers) so devices can find each other. This server does not intercept, log, or store your communication data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third-party advertisers or services, because we do not collect any data in the first place.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be indicated by an updated "Effective Date." Since we do not collect contact information, you are encouraged to periodically review this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official repository or support channels.
